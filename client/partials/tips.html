Top Tiger Monkey Paw Tips

Tip 1: Take notes in the description section - what did you learn from the video?

Tip 2: Put the timestamps of the parts you like the best in the description. Example:

  • 1:23 - start of materail new to me
  • 2:24 - I need to try this move

Tip 3: Use the "change order" feature to:

  • Keep the best videos at the top
  • Or, put the videos in the best viewing order
  • Or, keep track of which videos you most want to rewatch

Tip 4: Create new lists based on what you want to learn. Examples:

  • Basketball - low post moves
  • Basketball - ball handling
  • Basketball - defensive stance

Tip 5: Use the rating feature to keep track of which videos are the best.

Tip 6: Learning is best when it's shared. Find someone to talk about your subject with, find someone to teach, find someone to learn from.
